This project is a role-based Flask News Portal that leverages SQLite3 and SQLAlchemy to provide a comprehensive platform for managing and publishing news articles. It features a robust user management system with different roles (such as admin, publisher, and user), each with specific permissions. The portal supports article creation, categorization, and a workflow for drafting, submitting, and approving articles before publication. Users can comment on articles, and media files can be attached to enhance content. The application ensures content quality and security through an approval request system, making it suitable for collaborative news publishing environments where editorial oversight is required. Its modular structure and clear separation of concerns make it easy to extend and maintain.

Workout Sphere is a web-based fitness tracking application designed to help users manage and monitor their workout routines and progress. Built with Flask for the backend and SQLite for data storage, it offers features such as user registration, login, and profile management, allowing individuals to securely track their exercises and create personalized workout routines. The application also includes a social feed where users can share their workout achievements, fostering a sense of community and motivation. With a responsive interface powered by Tailwind CSS, Workout Sphere ensures a seamless experience across both mobile and desktop devices, making fitness tracking accessible and engaging for everyone.
